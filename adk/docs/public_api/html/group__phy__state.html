◆ headsetPhyState

enum headsetPhyState

#include <adk/src/domains/sensor/phy_state/headset_phy_state.h>

Enumeration of the physical states an Headset can be in.

Enumerator
HEADSET_PHY_STATE_UNKNOWN 

The headset physical state is unknown. This state value will not be reported to clients.

HEADSET_PHY_STATE_OFF_HEAD 

The headset is not on the head. It may be in motion or at rest.

HEADSET_PHY_STATE_OFF_HEAD_AT_REST 

The headset is not on the head, and no motion has been detected for configurable period of time

HEADSET_PHY_STATE_ON_HEAD 

The headset is on the head and usuable as a microphone and speaker.

enum phyState

#include <adk/src/domains/sensor/phy_state/phy_state.h>

Enumeration of the physical states an Earbud can be in.

Enumerator
PHY_STATE_UNKNOWN 

The earbud physical state is unknown. This state value will not be reported to clients.

PHY_STATE_IN_CASE 

The earbud is in the case.

PHY_STATE_OUT_OF_EAR 

The earbud is not in the case nor is it in the ear. It may be in motion or at rest.

PHY_STATE_OUT_OF_EAR_AT_REST 

The earbud is not in the case not is it in the ear, and no motion has been detected for configurable period of time

PHY_STATE_IN_EAR 

The earbud is in the ear and usuable as a microphone and speaker.
